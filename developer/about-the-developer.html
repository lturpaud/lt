About the Editor

Lazarus Turpaud is a Sophormore at Garfield High School. He "enjoys" running cross country and track for Garfield High School. He also loves the outdoors; whether it is backpacking around Mt. St. Helens, or going on an easy hike just outside Seattle, he is outside almost every weekend of the summer. He also volunteers for the Washington Trails Association, helping them repair trails for everyone to enjoy. When he is not outside, he is practicing piano, or developing code. He is also bilingual in English and Chinese. He chose to create an anthology of Afghan literature because he wanted to learn more about Afghan culture hidden behind the war-torn country.
